You are late for work, and your boss demands to know why. You...

  • Blame the bus, even though you cycle to work.
  • Blame the weather, even though it’s clear and sunny.
  • Play dead as if he was a predator, then run away and never look back.

It’s Valentine’s day and you forgot to buy your date a gift. You...

  • Buy a bow, stick it to your head and state you’re the present.
  • Order in sushi, hide all the boxes and claim you made it yourself.
  • Throw your phone to the ocean, run away and never look back.

You have 24 hours left to live. What do you do?

  • Give everybody you love and care a goodbye.
  • Throw yourself a pity party and invite your ex.
  • Buy a one way ticket to a Tropical island, run away and never look back.

It was a long and crazy day at work, and you just want to relax.

  • Call a friend to have a night out.
  • Hide under the blanket and cry yourself out.
  • Send a shameful txt to your boss, regret it a minute later, run away and never look back.

You were bitten by a spider on a school trip and apparently there are webs coming out of your wrist. You..

  • Search on google to find out you might have cancer.
  • Locate a potential criminal who has stolen a purse from an old lady, and tie him up.
  • Join the evil side, run away and never look back.
